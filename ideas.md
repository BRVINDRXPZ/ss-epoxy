**Design Movement:** Industrial Brutalism meets Liquid Metal

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw, heavy typography with extreme weight contrast — massive black headlines against whisper-thin subtext
2. Dark concrete-textured backgrounds with molten metallic accents (liquid gold/amber) that feel like poured epoxy
3. Asymmetric, off-grid layouts that break the expected — content bleeds into edges, overlaps images
4. Micro-interactions that feel tactile: hover states that "pour" color across elements

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Near-black charcoal `#0D0D0D` with subtle concrete grain texture
- Primary accent: Liquid amber/gold `#C8860A` — evokes poured epoxy resin
- Secondary: Warm white `#F5F0E8` for text
- Highlight: Electric copper `#E8724A` for CTAs
- Emotional intent: Power, permanence, craftsmanship — this floor will outlast you

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Hero: Full-bleed dark floor photo with massive left-anchored type, stats pinned to bottom-right
- Services: Diagonal slash dividers between sections, cards offset from grid
- Gallery: Masonry-style with variable heights, no uniform grid
- Contact: Split layout — dark left with contact info, light right with form

**Signature Elements:**
1. Diagonal "slash" motif — appears as section dividers, card corners, and button shapes
2. Liquid drip/pour animation on hover for CTA buttons
3. Grain/noise texture overlay at 4% opacity on all dark sections

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Scroll-triggered reveals: sections slide up from below with slight rotation
- Cursor: custom dot cursor that leaves a brief "epoxy trail"
- Hover on service cards: image zooms slightly, metallic overlay fades in

**Animation:**
- Hero text: staggered character-by-character reveal on load
- Stats counter: count-up animation when scrolled into view
- Gallery: items fade in with a slight scale from 0.95 to 1.0
- Section transitions: clip-path wipe from left to right

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Bebas Neue" — ultra-condensed, industrial, commanding
- Body: "DM Sans" — clean, modern, readable at small sizes
- Accent labels: "Space Mono" — monospace for stats and technical details

## Selected Design: Dark Industrial Craft — Concrete & Fire

Chosen for its perfect alignment with the epoxy flooring industry: dark backgrounds make floor photography look stunning, amber accents evoke the heat and craft of the process, and the bold asymmetric layout communicates confidence and professionalism without feeling generic.
